### 🧭 About Me

I'm a **Project / Program Manager** who lives at the intersection of **mobile OS, AI features, and cross-functional delivery**. Almost a decade of shipping consumer tech across India and APAC — currently at **vivo**, previously at **Xiaomi** and **Samsung**.

- 🎯 **What I do best:** turn fuzzy product ideas into shipped features with a roadmap, a spreadsheet, and a very stubborn follow-up cadence.
- 🤖 **What I'm obsessed with right now:** **Generative AI, LLMs, and LLM quality evaluation** — both how to ship them into real products, and how to study them (MCA in AI/ML, 2024).
- 🏗️ **What I'm quietly building on the side:** small **Java QA-automation** projects and **Google Colab notebooks** exploring deep learning and NLP — because managing engineers is easier when you've walked the debugger yourself.
- 🌏 **Languages:** English (professional) · Hindi (native) · Mandarin / 中文 (HSK 3, preparing for HSK 4).
